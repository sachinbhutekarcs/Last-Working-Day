About Last Working Day

Last Working Day - A brand by Volibits LLP. Last Working Day focusses on giving the opportunity to both the candidates (who are immediately available or are serving the notice period) seeking for a better opportunity and to the employers who are in dire need of such talents for their organization and projects. LWD helps in bridging the gap between the two by providing a platform with a pool of talented candidates and helping them join some of the most esteemed organisations.
